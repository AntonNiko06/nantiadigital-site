01

Overview

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what rights users have regarding their data when using the App.

The App is designed to function without collecting personal data from users.
02

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:

Names Email addresses Phone numbers Location data Device identifiers Usage analytics Advertising identifiers Contacts or address book data Photos, files, or media Health or fitness data

The developer of the App does not receive any user data.

03

Data Stored on Your Device

Some information created by the user within the App may be stored locally on the user's device in order for the App to function. Examples may include:

  • Tasks or entries created within the App
  • User preferences or settings

This data:

  • Remains only on the user's device
  • Is not transmitted to the developer
  • Is not stored on external servers
  • Is not accessible by the developer

If the App is uninstalled, locally stored data may be deleted by the operating system.

04

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use analytics or tracking technologies. Specifically, the App does not integrate any third-party analytics services, including but not limited to:

Google Analytics Firebase Analytics Mixpanel

Therefore, no information about user behavior, device usage, or interaction with the App is collected.

05

No Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use advertising services such as:

Google AdMob Facebook Audience Network

Because no advertising services are used, no advertising identifiers or marketing data are collected.

06

No Third-Party Data Sharing

The App does not share any user information with third parties. Since the App does not collect data, there is no information that can be sold, rented, shared, or disclosed.

07

Internet Connectivity

The App does not require internet connectivity for its core functionality and does not transmit user data over the internet.

08

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect any personal data from users of any age, no personal data from children is processed.

09

Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit user data, no personal information is stored on external servers controlled by the developer. All locally stored data remains under the control of the user on their own device.

10

Your Privacy Rights

Since the App does not collect or process personal data, the developer does not store any user information that could be accessed, modified, or deleted. Users remain in full control of any data stored locally on their own device.

11

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be published at the same policy location with an updated revision date.

Continued use of the App after any updates may constitute acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
